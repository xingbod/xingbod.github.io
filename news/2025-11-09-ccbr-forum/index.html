I was Invited to Give a Keynote Talk at CCBR 2025 | 我荣幸受邀在 CCBR 2025 作专题报告

Created on November 09, 2025

2025

[EN] CCBR 2025 | Biometric Security and Privacy Protection Forum

I was honored to be invited to give a keynote presentation at the CCBR 2025 Biometric Security and Privacy Protection Forum.

Talk Title: Key Technologies for Biometric Privacy Protection Abstract: This presentation provides a comprehensive overview of the attacks faced by biometric recognition systems and their corresponding defense methods. It focuses on reconstruction attacks and template attacks targeting both the image and feature domains, and discusses various defense strategies, including cancelable biometric transformation, fuzzy commitment schemes, biometric cryptosystems, and template protection schemes driven by Physical Unclonable Functions (PUF).

[CN] CCBR 2025 | 生物特征安全与隐私保护论坛

我荣幸受邀在 CCBR 2025 生物特征安全与隐私保护论坛作专题报告。

报告题目：生物识别隐私保护关键技术 报告摘要：本汇报对生物特征识别系统面临的攻击和相应的防御方法进行了全面概述。重点分析了针对图像域和特征域的重构攻击以及模板攻击，并讨论了多种防御策略，包括可撤销生物特征变形技术、模糊承诺方案、生物特征密码系统以及由物理不可克隆函数（PUF）驱动的模板保护方案。

Biography / 个人简介: Xingbo Dong (董兴波), Specially Appointed Associate Professor at Anhui University. His research focuses on biometric recognition security algorithms and image processing. He has presided over projects funded by NSFC and the Korean NRF Brain Pool Program. 安徽大学特聘副教授。研究聚焦生物特征识别安全保护算法、图像处理等。主持国家自然科学青年基金、韩国国家研究基金会Brain Pool Program等项目。
