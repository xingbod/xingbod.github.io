### [EN] NNICE 2026 | Keynote Presentation
I was honored to be invited to give a keynote presentation at the 2026 6th International Conference on Neural Networks, Information and Communication Engineering (NNICE 2026) in Hefei, China.

**Talk Title**: Advances and Challenges of Key Technologies for Biometric Privacy Protection

---

### [CN] NNICE 2026 | 大会主旨报告
我荣幸受邀在中国合肥举办的2026第六届神经网络、信息与通信工程国际学术会议（NNICE 2026）上作大会主旨报告。

**报告题目**：生物特征隐私保护关键技术的进展与挑战

---

**Biography / 个人简介**:
**Xingbo Dong (董兴波)**, Specially Appointed Associate Professor at Anhui University. His research focuses on biometric recognition security algorithms and image processing. He has presided over projects funded by NSFC and the Korean NRF Brain Pool Program.
安徽大学特聘副教授。研究聚焦生物特征识别安全保护算法、图像处理等。主持国家自然科学青年基金、韩国国家研究基金会Brain Pool Program等项目。
